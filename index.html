About Me

I am a self taught developer out of Brooklyn, New York. I am Jamaican-American and grew up in Lake Placid, New York. I graduated from SUNY New Paltz in 2017 with a BS in Environmental Geoscience. After college I moved to the city and began working as an environmental consultant. Even though I am on a successful career path in a field I studied in college, as soon as I started coding I realized that this is the field that I'm truly passionate about.


Like many people I always assumed that coding was too complex to pick up and that I clearly didn't fit the bill. Since I started I have been engrossed in the world and it has quickly become an important part of my life. I enjoy the challenges it brings and the opportunity to learn something new every day. I enrolled in the Columbia Full-Stack Web Development course and expect to graduate in June. Thank you for taking the time to learn a little bit about me!
